FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about funding speed, eligibility, and repayment structure.

If your case is delayed, our process is built to help you get paid faster.

What is pre-settlement funding?

Pre-settlement funding is a cash advance provided to plaintiffs with active personal injury cases, allowing them to access money before their case settles.

Do I have to repay if I lose my case?

No. Verde Assist Group offers non-recourse funding, meaning repayment is only required if you win or settle your case.

How fast can I get funded?

Many clients receive funding within 24 to 48 hours after approval, depending on case verification.

Do I need good credit?

No credit check is required. Approval is based on the strength of your case, not your credit score.

What types of cases qualify?

Common qualifying cases include car accidents, slip and falls, workplace injuries, and other personal injury claims.

How much funding can I receive?

Funding amounts vary based on your case details, but are typically a portion of your expected settlement.

Will this affect my lawsuit?

No. Funding does not interfere with your legal case or your attorney's strategy.

Do I need my attorney involved?

Yes. Your attorney helps provide case information needed to evaluate your eligibility.

Are there hidden fees?

No. All terms are clearly outlined in your agreement before you accept funding.

What can I use the money for?

Funds can be used for rent, bills, groceries, medical expenses, or any personal needs.

Do I make monthly payments?

No monthly payments are required. Repayment happens only if your case resolves successfully.

Is this a loan?

No. This is non-recourse funding, not a traditional loan.

Will applying affect my credit?

No. There is no impact on your credit score when applying.

Can I apply if I already have funding?

In some cases, additional funding may be available depending on your case status.

How do I apply?

You can apply by contacting us directly. We will guide you through a quick and simple process.
