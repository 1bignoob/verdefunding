FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about funding speed, eligibility, and repayment structure.

If your case is delayed, our process is built to help you get paid faster.

How quickly can I get approved?

Most complete submissions are reviewed within 24 to 48 hours. Timing depends on how quickly case records are available.

Do I have to repay if I lose?

Funding is typically non-recourse, which means repayment is usually made only when there is a recovery in your case.

Can I use funds for rent or medical bills?

Yes. Many clients use funds for household costs, treatment expenses, transportation, and other urgent financial needs.

Is attorney involvement required?

Yes. Your attorney generally helps provide case details and documentation needed for responsible review.

How much funding can I receive?

Amounts vary based on case specifics such as expected recovery value, liability, and timeline to resolution.
