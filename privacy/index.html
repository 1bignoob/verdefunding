Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2, 2025

Introduction

Verde Assist Group ("we", "us", "our") provides pre-settlement funding and related informational services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information collected through our website and related services. By using our website or submitting information to us, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

a) Information you provide directly: name, email address, phone number, mailing address, case details, messages you send through contact forms, and any documents or attachments you upload.

b) Technical and usage information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, pages viewed, referring/exit pages, and timestamps. This information is collected automatically via server logs and standard web technologies.

c) Cookies and similar technologies: We use cookies and local storage to improve site functionality, remember preferences, and for analytics. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and process contact form submissions.
  • Evaluate eligibility for pre-settlement funding and to communicate about offers and case-related questions.
  • Maintain and improve our website, products, and services.
  • Send administrative information and, where consented, marketing and promotional communications.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or other illegal activity.

How We Share Information

We may share personal information with service providers and vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., analytics providers, hosting, email delivery services), attorneys and third parties when necessary to assess a case or with your authorization, law enforcement or regulators when required by law, and in connection with business transactions such as mergers or acquisitions.

Data Security & Retention

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
