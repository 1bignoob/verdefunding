About Verde

Helping Plaintiffs Stay Financially Stable During Litigation

Verde Assist Group was built to support injury victims who need immediate financial breathing room while their legal case is still in progress. We understand the pressure of medical costs, rent, and daily expenses during long settlement timelines.

Our approach is simple: clear communication, rapid case review, and transparent non-recourse funding options designed around real client needs.

Our Core Commitments

  • Fast response times and direct communication
  • No hidden fee structures in offers
  • Respectful collaboration with your attorney
  • Multilingual support for better accessibility

Why Clients Choose Us

When your case timeline stretches, our team helps you access funding with speed and clarity.

Client-First Guidance

We explain every part of the process in plain language so you can make informed decisions confidently.

Attorney-Aligned Process

We coordinate with legal counsel to keep funding decisions aligned with case strategy.

Transparent Terms

You receive clear, documented terms before accepting any advance.
